# Smart Pets Marketplace Editing Rules

When editing this project:

## Always preserve:

* Two main homepage categories:

  * Interactive Pets
  * AI & Robotic Pets

## Audience priority:

1. Seniors
2. Adult children buying for parents
3. Gift buyers

## Homepage priorities:

* Keep homepage simple
* Use clear CTA buttons
* Avoid too many categories
* Keep trust high

## Product cards must always:

* Be visually clean
* Have consistent CTA wording
* Include clear product purpose

## CTA wording allowed:

* Shop Top Picks
* Compare Models
* View Details
* Explore Best for Seniors

## Never:

* Add toy-like styling
* Add cluttered menus
* Add more than 4 homepage product sections
* Use inconsistent category names

## Visual style:

* Premium affiliate site
* Similar quality level to premium review sites
* Senior-friendly readability

## Future expansion:

Allowed new sections:

* Best Interactive Cats
* Best Interactive Dogs
* Premium AI Pets
* Gift Guides

## Editing goal:

Every edit should improve:

* clarity
* trust
* conversion
* SEO readiness
